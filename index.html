What is Tow Truck Insurance?

Tow truck insurance is a specialised commercial motor insurance designed to protect towing businesses from the financial risks associated with vehicle recovery and roadside operations. It covers damage to your tow truck, liability for vehicles you tow, third-party injuries, public liability, and business interruptions caused by accidents or unexpected events.

Tow Truck Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive Motor Insurance

Coverage Range: $20,000 – $500,000+

(based on vehicle value)

Covers your tow truck for:

  • Accidental damage
  • Fire, theft, vandalism
  • Storm, flood, natural disasters
  • Collision and impact damage

Tow & Transport Liability (On-Hook / Hook Liability)

Coverage Range: $50,000 – $500,000 per towed vehicle

Covers customer vehicles while:

  • Winching
  • Loading / unloading
  • Lifting, towing, or transporting

Public Liability Insurance

Coverage Range: $5 Million – $20 Million

Protects your towing business against:

  • Injury to third parties
  • Property damage
  • Accidental incidents at recovery/accident sites

General Property / Tools of Trade Cover

Coverage Range: $10,000 – $100,000

Protects equipment such as:

  • Chains, dollies, towing tools
  • Jump starters, straps, accessories
  • Safety gear and uniforms

Business Interruption Insurance

Coverage Range: $1K – $5K per week (12–24 wks)

Compensates for income loss when your tow truck is off the road due to an insured event.

Worker’s Compensation

Mandatory Coverage

Mandatory for business owners with employees. Covers medical costs, injuries, rehabilitation.

Fleet Insurance

Customisable Rates

Available for businesses operating 2–50+ tow trucks, providing centralised management and discounted rates. Coverage is customised based on truck values and fleet size.
